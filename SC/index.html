25 PROFORMA OWNERS NAMED TO THE 2018 INC. 5000 LIST OF FASTEST-GROWING COMPANIES IN AMERICA

No other affiliate organization in our industry had even one.

Proforma is proud to announce that 25 Proforma Owners, totaling 12 businesses, have been named to Inc. Magazine's 2018 list of the 5,000 fastest-growing, private companies in America. With Proforma Owners experiencing business growth ranging from 66 to over 1,000 percent from 2014 to 2017, Proforma continues to hold the most franchise representation from a single organization on the list, as well as the greatest presence in the printing and promotional products industry.

"This is a great honor to be named once again to the most prestigious list of fastest growing companies in the U.S.," said Greg Muzzillo, Founder of Proforma. "We are proving year after year that we are a sustainable business, but perhaps more importantly a scalable one, helping owners reach their million and multi-million-dollar dreams through our extensive resource tools and support. We are very proud of these 25 Owners, representing 12 businesses, who continue to be committed to their success, and we are grateful for their contributions to Proforma's overall continued growth."
