Proforma Owners Awarded
5 of Top 6 Spots on
Fastest-Growing List

Each year, ASI’s Counselor publishes a list of movers and shakers in the industry who experience significant business growth. We’re proud to announce that a record-breaking, five members of our Proforma Family earned a spot on the top 10 list of the fastest-growing companies in 2018. No other franchise/affiliate organization had one member on the list.

PROFORMA AT THE TOP

Success and happiness can be defined in a variety of ways… whether it’s measured in time spent with family and friends, closing a big account or making the industry’s list of fastest-growing companies, members of the Proforma Family have the pleasure of experiencing it all.

KELLY STONE

The IdeaBox Powered by Proforma

THREE YEAR GROWTH: 212%

The IdeaBox Powered by Proforma has achieved 212% growth between 2015-17, with revenue in seven figures. Stone believes further sales acceleration is in the cards. “Our goal is to keep growing by being a true marketing partner that brings value to our clients,” she says.

MIKE DURKEE

Proforma Durkee

THREE YEAR GROWTH: 195%

In 2017, Proforma Durkee had 38% growth over the previous year. Since affiliating with Proforma in 2012, the company has seen sales increase by 1,175%. Proforma Durkee has expanded to a staff of 16, and the firm is projecting 2018’s growth to set another record for the company.

CHAS SHRAMEK

Proforma Connected

THREE YEAR GROWTH: 182%

Proforma Connected’s first big year came in 2016: both the company’s team and sales doubled, with a total revenue increase of 117%. The next year brought another 35% jump, elevating the company to just shy of 200% three-year growth. According to Shramek, 2018 is poised to keep that upward trend going.

RUSS HOWARTH

Proforma Think Ink

THREE YEAR GROWTH: 182%

Proforma Think Ink first became one of Counselor’s Fastest-Growing Companies in 2016. In 2017, the company’s sales rose by 42%. Over the last three full years, the firm’s sales have nearly tripled, and as of this past July, revenue for 2018 has already surpassed the 2017 figure.

MIKE LAZZARI

Proforma BrandCentric Solutions

THREE YEAR GROWTH: 171%

“I hit my biggest years starting in 2014,” Lazzari says. “That’s when I joined the Proforma Million Dollar Club and I have made it every year since. We just celebrated our first million-dollar month in February of this year.” Proforma Brand-Centric Solutions’ sales increased more than 100% between 2016 and 2017.

The #1 Distributor’s Secret to Success

Kelly Stone, Owner of The IdeaBox Powered by Proforma, engineered explosive growth between 2015 and 2017. The stellar performance earned the Florida-based distributorship the honor of Counselor’s Fastest-Growing Distributor for 2018.
